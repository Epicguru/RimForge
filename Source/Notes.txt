
Burning temperatures reference, in deg.C:

- Wood: 600 to 900, definitely not more than 1000.
- Chemfuel (based on gasoline): ~1000.
- Electrical (arc furnace): up to 2000 in practical use.

Melting points for common metals, rounded for simplification:
- Gold: 1000 deg.
- Steel (carbon steel): 1400 deg.
- Silver: 950 deg.
- Copper: 1100 deg.
- Tin: 231 deg.
- Uranium: 1100 deg.
- Plasteel (based on titanium): 1600 deg.
